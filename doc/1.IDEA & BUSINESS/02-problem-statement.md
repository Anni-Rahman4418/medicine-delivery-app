# Problem Statement

## Background

Access to medicines is a critical part of healthcare systems. However, in many regions, patients still depend on physical pharmacies to purchase required medicines.

---

## Problem Description

Many individuals face difficulties in accessing medicines due to:

* Lack of nearby pharmacies
* Physical illness or mobility issues
* Emergency situations
* Time constraints due to work or travel
* Long waiting times at pharmacies

---

## Core Problem

There is currently no simple, centralized, and user-friendly digital platform that allows patients to order medicines online, upload prescriptions, and receive deliveries efficiently.

---

## Impact of the Problem

Due to these limitations:

* Patients may delay treatment
* Elderly individuals struggle to access medicines
* Emergency medicine access becomes difficult
* Overall healthcare efficiency is reduced

---

## Need for a Solution

A digital medicine delivery system is needed to:

* Improve accessibility to medicines
* Reduce physical effort for patients
* Provide faster and more reliable service
* Connect users with pharmacies and delivery services

---

## Problem Summary

The absence of an integrated medicine delivery platform creates challenges in healthcare accessibility, efficiency, and convenience. This project aims to solve these issues through a mobile-based solution.
