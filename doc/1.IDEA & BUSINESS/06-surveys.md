# Surveys

## Introduction

A survey was conducted to collect data from potential users regarding their experience with medicine purchasing and their interest in a medicine delivery application.

---

## Survey Purpose

* To understand user behavior in buying medicines
* To identify common problems faced by users
* To evaluate demand for a medicine delivery system

---

## Survey Method

The survey was conducted using informal questionnaires shared with:

* Students
* Working individuals
* Elderly family members
* Local community members

---

## Sample Survey Questions

* How often do you buy medicines from a pharmacy?
* What difficulties do you face while purchasing medicines?
* Would you use a medicine delivery app if available?
* Which feature is most important to you? (Delivery, Prescription upload, Tracking, Price comparison)

---

## Survey Results Summary

### 1. Medicine Purchase Frequency

* Regular users: Moderate to high
* Occasional users: Majority group

---

### 2. Common Problems Identified

* Long waiting times at pharmacies
* Difficulty during illness or emergencies
* Lack of nearby pharmacies
* Transportation issues

---

### 3. Interest in Medicine Delivery App

* Majority of respondents showed strong interest
* High demand for home delivery service

---

### 4. Most Desired Features

* Home delivery of medicines
* Prescription upload system
* Order tracking
* Easy search functionality

---

## Analysis

The survey results clearly indicate a strong need for a digital medicine delivery system. Users prefer convenience, speed, and simplicity in accessing healthcare services.

---

## Conclusion

The survey confirms that a medicine delivery application like MediGo would be highly beneficial and widely accepted among users.
