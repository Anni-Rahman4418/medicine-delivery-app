# Interviews

## Introduction

Interviews were conducted to understand user requirements, challenges, and expectations regarding a medicine delivery system.

---

## Interview Objectives

* To understand difficulties faced in buying medicines
* To identify required features in a medicine delivery app
* To gather opinions from potential users and pharmacy staff

---

## Interview Participants

* General patients
* Elderly individuals
* Pharmacy staff

---

## Sample Interview Questions

### For Patients:

* How do you currently buy medicines?
* What problems do you face when visiting pharmacies?
* Would you prefer a medicine delivery app? Why?
* What features would be most useful for you?

---

### For Pharmacy Staff:

* How do you manage medicine orders currently?
* What challenges do you face in handling prescriptions?
* Would an online system help improve your workflow?
* What concerns do you have about digital medicine ordering?

---

## Key Responses Summary

### Patients:

* Difficulty in traveling to pharmacies
* Time-consuming process
* Interest in home delivery service
* Need for easy prescription upload feature

---

### Pharmacy Staff:

* Manual order handling is inefficient
* Prescription verification is important
* Need for better inventory tracking
* Interest in automated order management system

---

## Findings from Interviews

* Strong demand for medicine delivery services
* Users want simple and fast mobile apps
* Prescription handling is a critical requirement
* Pharmacies need digital order management tools

---

## Conclusion

The interviews confirmed that a medicine delivery application is highly useful and necessary. The feedback collected helped define the core features of the MediGo system.
