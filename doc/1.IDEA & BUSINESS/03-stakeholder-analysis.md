# Stakeholder Analysis

## Introduction

Stakeholders are individuals or groups who are directly or indirectly affected by the Medicine Delivery System (MediGo) or have an interest in its development and success.

---

## Primary Stakeholders

### 1. Patients / Users

* Main users of the application
* Use the system to search, order, and receive medicines
* Depend on the system for fast and reliable access to medicines

**Needs:**

* Easy-to-use interface
* Fast delivery
* Prescription upload support
* Order tracking

---

### 2. Pharmacies

* Provide medicines through the platform
* Manage inventory and approve orders

**Needs:**

* Easy inventory management system
* Order notifications
* Prescription verification system

---

### 3. Delivery Personnel

* Responsible for delivering medicines to users

**Needs:**

* Clear delivery instructions
* Navigation support
* Order status updates

---

## Secondary Stakeholders

### 4. System Administrator

* Manages the overall system
* Controls users, pharmacies, and orders

**Needs:**

* Dashboard for monitoring system activity
* Control over data and access
* Reports and analytics

---

### 5. Healthcare Professionals (Optional)

* May provide prescription validation or recommendations

**Needs:**

* Access to prescription data (if permitted)
* Secure system access

---

## Tertiary Stakeholders

### 6. Government / Regulatory Bodies

* Ensure compliance with healthcare and medicine regulations

**Needs:**

* Safe and legal medicine distribution
* Proper prescription handling

---

## Stakeholder Summary

The MediGo system involves multiple stakeholders including patients, pharmacies, delivery staff, and administrators. Each group plays a critical role in ensuring the system functions effectively and provides reliable healthcare services.
